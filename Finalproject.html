My game is called Whack A Bitmoji. In this game you are supposed to
hit or whack a bitmoji to increase your score. To start out, you need to click the link to go to the game page.
Once at the game page, you can click the start button to begin playing. Once you start playing you need to click
the Khan Critter as many times as you can. After you reach a score of ten, you'll go to the next level. At the next level
you'll see the screen go red and a timer start clicking down. After the timer reaches 0, you are met with an end screen.
